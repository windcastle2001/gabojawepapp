# Monetization And Marketing

Last updated: 2026-05-03

## Monetization Decision

Subscription should be the primary monetization model. Ads are a poor fit for a private couple space because they make the product feel less intimate and less trustworthy.

Recommended model:

- Free plan:
  - One couple space.
  - Limited saved places and visits.
  - Limited media storage.
  - Limited AI recommendations per month.
- Premium plan:
  - Unlimited or much higher saved places.
  - Expanded media storage.
  - Couple memory database.
  - Advanced AI date planning.
  - Anniversary, MBTI, zodiac, and preference-aware suggestions.
  - Ranking and "our best places" features.
  - Travel/date recommendations across languages.

Recommended pricing experiment:

- Monthly: 2,900-4,900 KRW.
- Annual: 29,000-39,000 KRW.
- Couple-based entitlement: if one partner pays, both can use premium in that couple space.

Trial recommendation:

- Prefer a free plan plus a short premium trial, such as 7 days.
- Avoid relying on a 1-month free trial as the main hook because users may forget the product before the habit forms.

## Secondary Revenue

Later, after meaningful usage:

- Date place reservation affiliates.
- Flower, cake, gift, and anniversary affiliate links.
- Travel, hotel, restaurant, and activity partnerships.
- Paid AI date plan packs.
- Couple memory reports, photo books, PDFs, and physical goods.

## Marketing Positioning

Do not market as another "date place recommendation map." That category is crowded.

Better positioning:

> The AI date companion that remembers your couple.

Alternative hooks:

- "For couples who always ask: where should we go this weekend?"
- "A private map of your dates, memories, and tastes."
- "Kakao has your chat. Gajago has your memories and plans."
- "An AI date planner that knows your budget, weather, location, and preferences."

## Early Channels

- TikTok, Reels, Shorts.
- Couple vloggers and date-course creators.
- Instagram local date accounts.
- University communities and early couple communities.
- Japan/Taiwan: Instagram, TikTok, X.

## First Growth Experiments

1. Create a landing page with waitlist.
2. Test three messages:
   - AI date planner.
   - Couple memory map.
   - Anniversary/date assistant.
3. Measure signup, save, invite, and first shared place.
4. Use the winner to prioritize MVP features.
